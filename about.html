The Last.fm collage generator

It allows you to create a collage of all the albums, songs or artists you have been listening to over different time spans. Then you can share that image with your friends or just get a better feel for what kind of music you really listen to! You can even create collages that include your friend's accounts as well!

It's easy to use! All you have to do is enter in your last.fm username and then hit the generate button!
After that you can click on any of the pictures to take you to the last.fm page to learn more about it or you can convert it to an image to easily share with friends.
